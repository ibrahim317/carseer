Glossary

Ownership/Registration: Vehicle registration is a standared procedure worlwide to use vehicle legally. Checking vehicle's historical ownership records is important in the proper assessment of the used vehicle. Experts prefer as few former owners as possible, but some cases are an exception to this rule.

Recalls: Recalls can be iniated by manufacturer or the parties who administer traffic safety. This is to inform owners of vehicle regarding defects or safety improvements were suggested, and in most cases it cost nothing at owners.

Data may be updated: The CARSEER report sources for obtaining used vehicle records are reliable. However, new records may be added to the vehicle after extracting its report due to reasons for delaying transactions from the source. CARSEER alerts users of most of these cases to protect them from any misleading information or importing cars that are not allowed to be registered according to local market regulations.
